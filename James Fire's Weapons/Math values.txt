Weapon damage is 4x the top-tier vanilla weapon.

Cost is 2x, with 25% in the relevant SR. 3x if it doesn't use an SR.

Reactor power output is 4x.

Armour/Health bonus is 4x.

Energy Upkeep is 2% of cost, alloy upkeep is 1% of cost. Based on alloy cost

SR upkeep is 1% of the SR cost, if applicable.

Power usage is 4x.

Shield regen is 5% of shields, instead of being based on vanilla numbers

Shield Armour is this stuff compared to the shield generator, except alloy and sr cost are swapped

Weapons go an additional 25% damage step in vs shields and armour, becoming more specialized against their intended target.
