A mechanic by which to represent how unified your population is, and how easily you can direct them to do what you want. Happier people are more pliable. Fear works too.

Cohesion Malus affected by Planets(10, Reduced by FTL tech, and eliminated entirely if there is a gateway in the system), Pops(1, less if they have your governing ethics, more if they don't.), Systems(5, Reduced by FTL tech, and eliminated entirely if there is a gateway in the system), Planetary Happiness(1 for each 5% below 50), Civics(5-10), Governors/Generals on planets(From bad traits), Ruler(From bad traits), Faction Approval/Support(1 for each 1% below 50, modified by support value).

Cohesion Bonus affected by Unity Production(1), Enforcers(3), Soldiers(5 With Martial Law active.), Planetary Happiness(1 for each 5% above 50), Civics(5-10), Traditions(5-10), Sectors(5 per planet in the sector.), Governors/Generals on planets(1 per level, plus good traits), Ruler(10 per level, plus good traits), Faction Approval/Support(1 for each 1% above 50, modified by support value).

Minus Malus from Bonus, then:

For each point of Cohesion under 100(Capped at -100), lose 1 Stability, -1% Governing Ethics Attraction, +1% Sprawl from Pops.

For each point of Cohesion over 100(Capped at 100), gain 1% Resources from Jobs, 1% Governing Ethics Attraction, -1% Sprawl from Pops, 1 Piracy Suppression/Trade Protection (Depending on if I can do the first).
