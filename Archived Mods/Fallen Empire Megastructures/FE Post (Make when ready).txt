Alrighty. I'm starting the first phases of making a mod to enhance FE flavour and rewards.

And I want feedback on the rough ideas. Balance suggestions, ideas for other things we could do (we are lacking in things for the Spiritualist FE, basically defaulting to 'Make Psionics better for them').

Right now, compatibility with [Massive Sol System](http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=919932376&searchtext=Massive), [Real Space](http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=937289339), and [GigaStructural Engineering and More](http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1121692237&searchtext=gigastructural) is planned. Feedback on these choices is welcome, and other mods that may be wanting compatibility are welcome.

(Planned) Feature List:

Xenophobe Economic Powerhouse / Genocidal Maniacs

Xenophile Enigmatic Observers / Caretakers

Materialist Keepers of Knowledge / Disinterested Videogamers

Spiritualist God Among Us / Fallen Portal 

Economic Powerhouse- 

Vanilla = Dyson Sphere (Unfinished or damaged)

Giga = Dyson Sphere (Unfinished or damaged), Neutronium GigaForge (Unfinished or disrepaired), 
50% random Chance for each of either the physics or engineering (Both of which are functional)

Awaken- First priority is to fix/finish their things

Genocidal Maniacs-

Giga = Nicoll-Dyson Beam (Damaged)

Awaken- First priority is to fix their Genocide cannon

Observers-

Vanilla = Sentry Array (Unfinished or damaged)

Caretakers- Virtual Reality Simulator

God Among Us-

Vanilla = Chosen One (Buffed!)

Fallen Portal-

Giga = Hypersiphon (Inactive) Awaken- May activate Hypersiphon

Keepers of Knowledge-

Vanilla = Science Nexus (Special FE Version)

Giga = Matrioshka Brain (Unfinished or disrepaired) Awaken- First priority is to fix/finish their brain

Disinterested Videogamers-

Giga = Virtual Reality (Finished)  -------- Virtual Reality Brain Full of pops, with a ringworld to provide the minerals and food.

Feature requests are welcome, but be warned they may not make it, either due to being too hard, or just plain impossible to do. Anything reasonable will be, at the very least, considered.

Ask away!
