Overhauls ship design to be more sensible, have more options, and present better choices. Overall, ships should be more powerful, and more expensive.

Overall, utility slots have been changed to represent internal components of the ship.

Armour is now a required component, with a second one to denote armour thickness, completely removing armour from utility slots.

Shield Generators are now a required component on ships, as well as an Aux component. These add flat shield to the ship. Shield Capacitors are now the normal utility components, giving a multiplier to the flat shield from the Generators. The more power storage dedicated to shields, the more powerful they are. The Shield Capacitor Aux component has been removed.

The Reactor required component now represents the Primary power source of the ship. You can add secondary reactors to normal component slots. The Reactor Booster Aux component has been removed.

Ships now have a Hull Material required component, replacing their base health (Except for Starbases, they get both their base and the new component) with a component based on your materials technology.

Adds Weapon Capacitor utility components, that increase weapon fire rate, and weapon damage.

Ship sections with utility slots can be shifted into offence or utility, exchanging 1 weapon/utility slot for the other one. This allows focusing ships more on offensive power, or defensive power.

Allowed regen at all times.

All weapons have had their base damage doubled.

Ships require 2x the Naval cap.

Starbases have a 1.5x range modifier

Overhauled weapon damage scaling.*
