Beefs up starbases and introduces a few new buildings. For Patch 2.7

[h1]Full List of Changes:[/h1]

[b]Starbases[/b]
- New T6 Starbase Stellar Bastion, gives 12 module and 8 building slots, 24 platform slots, twice the hp and armor as a citadel, has access to a titan weapon slot (with Apocalypse only)
- Star Fortress gives 4 building slots
- Citadel gives 9 module slots and 6 building slots, 18 platform slots
- Starbases get +1 medium gun slots for all levels
- Starbases get +2 utility slots for all levels
- All starbases except outposts get +1 hanger slot
- Starhold and Star Fortress gets +1 guided slot and +1 large slot, Citadel and Bastion gets +2 of each
- Citadel gets +1 extra large gun slot, Bastion gets +2
- Starbases get point defense slots, ranging from 1 for outposts to 10 for bastions
- Star Fortress and Citadel get +1 auxiliary component slot
- Juggernauts now also get 1 titan weapon and 3 large weapons
- Juggernauts now have +30,000 base armor

[b]Starbase Modules[/b]
- Trading hubs give +2 trade value, and +1% system trade value
- Trading hubs get an additional +2 trade value if built in a system with a trading enclave
- The weapon modules give +1 weapon slot each, hanger bay also gives +1 point defense slot
- Solar Panels give 4 energy

[b]Starbase Buildings[/b]
- NEW: Pulsar Observatory, +15 engineering, requires level 3 sensor tech
- NEW: Interstellar Customs Office, gives +1 trade collection and protection range, +10 trade protection, +5 trade value, and +5 society research. Can only be built in systems bordering a country with whom you have a commercial pact. Requires Intergalactic Customs, not available to gestalts and criminal syndicates
- New: Syndicate Free Haven, gives +1 protection range, +20 trade protection, +10 trade value. Requires Criminal Syndicate civic. Can only be built in systems bordering a country that has one of your branch offices on one of their planets. Requires Intergalactic Customs
- NEW: Trading post, gives +8 minerals, +2 alloys. Requires Gestalt ethics. Can only be built in systems bordering a country that has open borders with you. Requires Intergalactic Trade
- NEW: Stellar Fortifications, gives +25% shields and armor, requires starbase fortification I
- NEW: Patrol Office, doubles trade protection and protection range from the three military modules. Requires Interstellar Enforcement
- NEW: Dathnak Embassy, gives +10 society research, +10 trade/energy, can only be built in systems with Dathnak colonies
- NEW: Atmospheric Manipulator, gives +25% food production, +15% habitability, requires Terrestrial Sculpting
- NEW: Resource Processing Center, gives +100% mining station output, requires Nanite Mineral Probes
- NEW: Minelayer Hanger, 20% chance per day to cause 10-25 damage, ignoring 75% of armor
- Offworld Trading Company gives +1% system trade value per trading hub
- Resource Silos give +4000 storage
- Deep Space Black Site gives +3 unity
- Artist College gives +20 unity
- Proxy Office gives +10 trade
- Hydroponics Bay gives 6 food
- Black Hole Observatory gives +15 physics research, can be built in systems with a natural wormhole
- Curator Think Tank gives +10 of each research type
- Command Center gives +5 tracking
- Communications Jammer gives enemy ships -5 tracking
- Defense Grid Supercomputer gives +10 defense platform capacity, up from +6
- Disruption Field Generator gives enemy ships -25% shields, up from -20%
- Hyperlane Registrar gives +20% sublight speed, +1 trade protection and collection
- Target Uplink Computer gives +5 tracking
For Real Space 3.0:
- NEW: Protostar observatory, built around young stars, +15 physics, requires level 2 sensor tech
- Nebula Refinery can be build in any system with a local nebula or proto-planetary disk
- Black Hole Observatory can be built around microquasars

[b]Technologies[/b]
- NEW: Interstellar Enforcement tech, tier 2, +5 trade protection, gives access to Patrol Office starbase building
- NEW: Intergalactic Customs, tier 2, +5% trade value, +5% diplomatic weight from economy, gives access to Interstellar Customs Office starbase building
- NEW: Intergalactic Trade, tier 2, -5% market fee, +5% diplomatic weight from economy, gives access to Trading Post Office starbase building, Gestalt only.
- NEW: Orbital Gene Crops, tier 3 tech, +4 food from orbital hydroponics, +1 farmer jobs from hydroponic farms on habitats. Requires Gene Crops and Hydroponics
- NEW: Improved Photovoltaics, rare tier 3 tech, Solar Panel Network gives an additional +2 energy
- NEW: Galactic Trading Company, rare tier 4 tech, Offworld Trading Company gives an additional +2 trade value to trading hubs
- NEW: Interstellar Fleet Doctrines, rares tier 4 tech, Naval Logistic Office gives an additional +2 naval capacity to anchorages
- NEW: Positronic Defense Algorithms, tier 5, Defense-Grid Supercomputer gives an additional +6 defense platform capacity
- NEW: Three engineering techs that increase starbase hull points by +15/+20/+25%. Third tech also increases the bonus for the Stellar Fortifications building and adds daily armor and shield regen
- NEW: Dark Matter Extraction, rare tier 5 tech, Black Hole Observatories give +1 dark matter, requires Dark Matter Drawing
- NEW: Zero-G Foundries, rare tier 3 tech, +4 alloys from Nebula Refineries, requires Autonomous Mining Drones
- NEW: Eternal Vigilance, rare tier 4 tech, +25% governing ethics attraction, Deep Space Black Site gives an additional +3 unity, -10 crime, +2 ruler political power, requires The Living State and The Collective Self
- NEW: Shipyard Optimization, rare tier 4 tech, +25% shipyard construction speed, requires Corps of Engineers
- Space mining and research techs increase the bonus of starbase buildings
- Improved Structural Integrity gives +10% starbase hull points
- Synchronized Defenses tech gives +1 starbase capacity
- Juggernaut tech now require Stellar Bastion tech

[b]Other[/b]
- +1 starbase capacity per 200 pops
- Ai should be more capable at building starbases
