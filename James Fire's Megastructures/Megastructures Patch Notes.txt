Tweaks to all the megastructures that I am using as a personal mod. I uploaded it in case others want them.

CHANGES:

Dyson Sphere energy production x4

Dyson Sphere mineral cost toned down slightly.

Dyson Sphere and Science Nexus limit removed. You can build as many as you want.

Ringworld sections no longer block each others' construction.

Science Nexus now gives 1% research speed at max level, with a 2% if you get the special event for it.

Science Nexus and Habitats can now be built on moons.

Added Military Academy Megastructure, requiring master builders to construct, and producing Naval Cap.
